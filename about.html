About MEA Chyme

The Messaging Space for Event People

Our Mission

MEA Chyme was created to solve a real problem in the event industry - the need for a dedicated messaging platform that understands the unique needs of event professionals. We believe that event teams deserve better communication tools that are designed specifically for their workflow.

What We Do

We provide a comprehensive messaging platform that allows event teams to:

  • Connect seamlessly with team members and crew
  • Form groups for different aspects of event planning
  • Host virtual meetings and discussions
  • Share events, connections, and experiences
  • Collaborate on event-related content and photos

Our Story

MEA Chyme was born from the frustration of event professionals who were using generic messaging apps that didn't understand their specific needs. Whether you use My Event Advisor's event management platform or others, MEA Chyme provides the communication layer that brings your entire event ecosystem together.

Our Values

Event-Focused

Everything we build is designed with event professionals in mind. We understand your workflow and create tools that enhance it.

Secure & Reliable

Your event communications are secure and reliable. We prioritize the privacy and security of your team's conversations.

Innovation

We continuously innovate to provide the best messaging experience for event teams, staying ahead of industry needs.

Our Team

The people behind MEA Chyme are passionate about improving event industry communication.

Event Industry Experts

Our team includes professionals with years of experience in event planning, management, and technology.

Technology Innovators

We combine deep technical expertise with a passion for creating user-friendly solutions for event professionals.

Community Builders
