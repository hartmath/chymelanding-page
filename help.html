Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download MEA Chyme?

MEA Chyme is available for download on both iOS and Android devices. Visit the App Store or Google Play Store and search for "MEA Chyme" to download the app. The download is free, and you'll pay the one-time fee of $10.99 after installation.

How much does MEA Chyme cost?

MEA Chyme costs $10.99 as a one-time fee for unlimited access to all features. This is a limited-time offer with no monthly subscriptions or hidden costs.

Can I use MEA Chyme with other event management platforms?

Yes! MEA Chyme is designed to work alongside any event management platform, including My Event Advisor and others. It's a standalone messaging platform that complements your existing event management tools.

How do I create a team in MEA Chyme?

To create a team, tap the "+" button in the main interface, select "Create Team," and follow the setup wizard. You can then invite team members by email or phone number.

Is my data secure on MEA Chyme?

Yes, we take security seriously. All your messages are end-to-end encrypted, and we implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data and privacy.

Can I host video meetings in MEA Chyme?

Yes! MEA Chyme includes built-in video meeting capabilities. You can start a video call directly from any team chat or schedule meetings in advance.
