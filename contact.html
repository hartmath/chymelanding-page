Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with MEA Chyme?

Simply download the app from the App Store or Google Play, create your account, and start connecting with your event team!

Is there a free trial?

MEA Chyme offers a one-time fee of $10.99 for unlimited access to all features. No monthly subscriptions or hidden costs.

Can I use MEA Chyme with other event management platforms?

Yes! MEA Chyme is designed to work alongside any event management platform, including My Event Advisor and others.

How secure is my data?

We take security seriously. All your communications are encrypted and secure. Your data privacy is our priority.
